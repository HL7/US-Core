Name:OmbEthnicityCategories
Title:OMB Ethnicity Categories
Status:Active as of 5/21/19
Definition:

The codes for the concepts 'Unknown', 'Asked but no answer', and the ethnicity categories - 'Hispanic or Latino' and 'Non Hispanic or Latino' - as defined by the OMB Standards for Maintaining, Collecting, and Presenting Federal Data on Race and Ethnicity, Statistical Policy Directive No. 15, as revised, October 30, 1997.
