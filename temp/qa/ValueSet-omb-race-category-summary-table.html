Name:OmbRaceCategories
Title:OMB Race Categories
Status:Active as of 12/8/21
Definition:

The codes for the concepts 'Unknown', 'Asked but no answer', 'other, and the codes for the five race categories - 'American Indian' or 'Alaska Native', 'Asian', 'Black or African American', 'Native Hawaiian or Other Pacific Islander', and 'White' - as defined by the OMB Standards for Maintaining, Collecting, and Presenting Federal Data on Race and Ethnicity, Statistical Policy Directive No. 15, as revised, October 30, 1997 .
