Name:USCoreConditionCategoryCodes
Title:US Core Condition Category Codes
Status:Active as of 11/30/21
Definition:

The US Core Condition Category Codes support the separate concepts of problems, health concerns, and Social Determinants of Health in Condition.category in order for API consumers to be able to separate them.
